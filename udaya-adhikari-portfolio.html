Reflection

What I Have Learned

Looking back on my growth from first year to second year at Metropolia.

Biggest Growth

"I have gone from writing my first lines of code to building complete apps that connect to real APIs and work for real users."

From 1st to 2nd Year

In first year I learned the basics of programming and software development. Now in second year, I can plan, build, and test a full mobile application — and I understand why each decision matters, not just how to write the code.

Most Challenging Topics

API integration and understanding how data flows between a mobile app and a backend server were difficult at first. I overcame this by reading documentation carefully, testing step by step, and asking for help when needed.

Group Projects & Teamwork

Working in team projects taught me more than solo assignments. I improved my communication, learned to use Git for collaboration, and understood how to divide responsibilities fairly in a diverse team.

Studying in Finland

Studying at Metropolia in an international environment has been a great experience. Working with classmates from different countries has improved my English, broadened my perspective, and shown me how to collaborate across cultural differences.
